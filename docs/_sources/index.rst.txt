Cassini
-------

Cassini is a virtual lab-book framework, using Jupyter Lab and Python.

Its aim is to help you structure, store and explore your data.

It's fully customisable, but provides a powerful out-of-the-box configuration.

Try it out in .. image:: https://mybinder.org/badge_logo.svg
 :target: https://mybinder.org/v2/gh/0Hughman0/Cassini/master?filepath=lab%2Ftree%2Fexamples%2Fdefault%2FHome.ipynb
